GHOST - 星街すいせい Cosplay Music Video

The "GHOST" Cosplay Music Video was based on Hoshimachi Suisei's third-anniversary original song. Built around the question of whether applause is directed at the persona, the name, or the person behind it, the project used Unreal Engine 4 and virtual production workflows to create symbolic music visuals.
