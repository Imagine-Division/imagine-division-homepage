TOP NOVA 2023

Top Nova Music Festival 2023 was an outdoor live music event held at Kwun Tong Promenade, organised by Imagine Division Ltd. and co-organised with the Hong Kong Guangxi Association and the Hong Kong Guangxi Kowloon East Service Center. The event featured nearly 30 local singers and bands across diverse musical styles.
