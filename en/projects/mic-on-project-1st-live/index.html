《Mic On Project 1st Live》

MOP 1st Live Concert was one of Hong Kong's first music live concerts to integrate XR technology and bring VTubers into a physical stage environment. Presented by Imagine Division Ltd. and co-organised with Mic On Project, the event also featured a collaborative performance with Hong Kong local band But Nothing Happened, combining virtual characters, live music, and stage performance.
